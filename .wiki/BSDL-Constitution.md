*The design language itself: what BSDL is, and where the canonical text lives.*

**BSDL — the Britx Software Design Language** — is the constitutional layer of this repository: the worldview, values, principles, and engineering laws from which everything else (including BSF, the operating framework this wiki mostly describes) is derived.

> Software is the intentional organisation of human knowledge into executable systems that transform reality in service of humanity.

## Where the truth lives

The canonical, ratified text lives **in the repository**, not in this wiki: [`bsdl/`](https://github.com/pedrobritx/bsdl/tree/main/bsdl). This page is a map, and it must never contradict the canon.

## BSDL vs BSF

BSDL states *why* and *what must hold*; BSF (the rest of this wiki) encodes *how a project operates day to day*. When they conflict, the constitution wins, and the conflict is resolved through an ADR — see [ADR-001](https://github.com/pedrobritx/bsdl/blob/main/decisions/adr/001-bsdl-canon-three-surfaces.md) for how the repo, this wiki, and the future website relate.
