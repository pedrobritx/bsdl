*The front door: what the Britx Software Framework is, and where everything lives.*

> **The repository is the company. Humans and AI are just different kinds of employees.**

The Britx Software Framework (BSF) is an AI-native operating system for designing, building, shipping, and maintaining software. Not a template, not a prompt collection: a governance and knowledge structure where the repository itself holds the company's memory, standards, roles, and decision process — so context survives conversations, sessions, and model changes.

**Who is this for?** Builders who work with AI development tools (Claude Code, Codex, Gemini CLI, Cursor, Amp — BSF is model-agnostic) and are tired of re-explaining their project every session, relearning old mistakes, and losing the "why" behind decisions. If you want every project to start from established standards and every decision to be traceable, this is your front door.
